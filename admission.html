The Principal

I am overwhelmed with joy to see new student entering into clegg girls senior high school. The journey of a thousand mile begin with a step. It gladdens my heart to see that from a humble beginning of a student population. the school has made remarkable progress not only in numerical strength but also in quality of instruction, physical structures and facilities, the latest being the introduction of provision of internet facility, face lift for our computer lab, library and other special rooms while procurement of additional sporting equipment is not left out. The newly introduced corporate guards have also made a lot of difference in the security of life and property in the compound.The completion of secondary school education is another milestone in the academic pursuit.
