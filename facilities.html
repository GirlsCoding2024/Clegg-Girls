School Facilities

The Clegg Girls network encompasses approximately 12 school facilities that are not only operational but also thriving, serving as vital hubs of education for students. These facilities are actively utilized by students, contributing to their academic growth and overall development.
ICT LAB laptop_windows

Our ICT Lab is a dynamic space designed to empower students with essential digital skills and knowledge, preparing them for success in the modern world.

PHYSICS LAB lightbulb_outline

Our physics lab is a hub of scientific inquiry, and discovery, providing students with the tools they need to unravel the secrets of the physical world.

CHEMISTRY LAB bubble_chart

Our Chemistry Lab, where molecules dance, reactions sizzle, and discoveries abound. It is a haven for budding scientists, providing a space for impact.

BIOLOGY LAB pregnant_woman

Our lab is a vibrant hub of scientific inquiry and discovery, where students embark on a journey to explore the wonders of the natural world

AGRIC LAB local_florist

It is a fertile ground for exploration, providing students with experiences of agriculture and its vital role in feeding the world today.

CATERING LAB local_dining

We bring together techniques to create unforgettable dining experiences, our lab facility serves as a hub for culinary creativity.

STAFF ROOM people

This digital space is designed to keep our valued staff connected, informed, and inspired as we work together towards our shared goals.

PRINCIPAL'S OFFICE person

This space is designed as a frame welcome message to greet visitors as they enter, it is a place where school mission statement are emphasized.

FOOD VENDOR store

Our shool food vendor is a cafeteria where students buy food,snacks and drink of their choice to satisfy their hunger and thirst.

EKO PROJECT ROOM event_seat

Our Eko project room is a fantastic idea to showcase your initiatives, share progress and engage with stakeholders for environmental sustainability.

SICK BAY local_hospital

Our sick bay creates a supportive and well equipped environment that promotes the health and well-being of everyone in our school community.

LIBRARY book

Our library contains collections of books, periodicals and sometimes films and recorded music for use or borrowing by members of the school.
