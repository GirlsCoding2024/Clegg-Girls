About us

Cleggs Girls Senior High School stands as a beacon of educational excellence within Lagos State, recognized for its commitment to nurturing tomorrow's leaders. Since its inception, our institution has remained synonymous with academic distinction and a steadfast dedication to shaping young minds for a brighter future.

Driven by a profound mission to make a positive impact on the world, Cleggs Girls Senior High School endeavors to instill in its students a sense of responsibility, empathy, and global citizenship. Through a robust curriculum and a rich tapestry of extracurricular activities, we empower our students to become compassionate leaders and agents of change in their communities and beyond.

School Anthem

At Clegg Girls Senior High School, we raise our banner high to train our girls to be self-reliant and to make the nation grow. With God on our side, we will reach the heights God leads, and we follow.

Dress Code

The dress code at Clegg Girls Senior High School is not merely a matter of attire; it's a symbol of our identity and values. The elegant combination of white and wine embodies the essence of our community, weaving together layers of meaning that resonate with our students, faculty, and alumni.

White:
With its pristine hue, symbolizes purity and clarity of purpose. It reflects our commitment to integrity, honesty, and academic excellence.

Wine:
Complementing the purity of white is the rich, deep tone of wine. This color represents sophistication, maturity, and passion. In the wine-colored accents of our dress code, we find echoes of the intellectual curiosity and creative spirit that drive our students to excel both inside and outside the classroom.
